Common Vampire Bat

Vampire bats have anesthesia in their saliva, so when they bite an animal with their sharp teeth, the animal usually doesn't feel it!

  • Scientific Name: Desmodus rotundus
  • Average Length: 9 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: Caves, trees, and abandoned buildings in tropical climates

Vampire bats are the creatures that haunt little children's (and some adults') nightmares. Their primary diet consists of blood from cows, horses, and other mammals. They hunt exclusively at night, and go as far as 5 miles from their roost!
